Our company

Founded in 1993, ETC Group is a leader in Telecom Materials Distribution and Supply Chain Optimization Solutions for Operators and Contractors throughout Europe and North America.

The acquisition of ETC by Carlyle Group in 2019 has positioned us to significantly grow our products, services and presence globally.
Our years of expertise spans end to end the supply chain work flow from product design, procurement, materials distribution and logistics operations.
