About
ETC GROUP

Haec igitur prima lex amicitiae sanciatur, ut ab amicis honesta petamus, amicorum causa honesta faciamus, ne exspectemus quidem, dum rogemur

The group is a leader and the critical link between the sourcing and distribution of equipment and materials for the telecom market in Europe and in the US

Founded in 1993, ETC Group is a leading actor in EMEA (France, Portugal and Israël) and in the US, in the design, procurement and distribution of materials, tooling and equipments used by subcontractors to install and maintain telecommunications infrastructure networks to the building and to the home.

One of the key markets where ETC Group has unparalleled expertise is in the “Superfast Broadband” fibre networks: end-to-end FttH (fibre to the home) and HFC (hybrid fibre-coaxial), the latter of which includes FttB (fibre to the building plus coaxial cable for the last small section to the home)

Over more than 25 years, ETC Group has forged strong relationships with all levels of the value chain: telecom operators, national specialist telecom contractors, civil engineering groups and local SME subcontractors, bringing unequalled expertise in co-development, sourcing, and procurement of innovative products.

The success of ETC Group’s business model lies in its incumbent position as the critical link in the sourcing of product and equipment needs for the full value chain, from the telecom operator who prescribes the products to all the various levels of direct and indirect sub-contractors.
