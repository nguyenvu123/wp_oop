Comments

  • The four product families (1 to 4 below) Optical Cable, Optical Accessories, Optical Rack and Hybrid Fiber Coaxial (HFC) are products (materials and components) referenced with a given network operator : they mainly represent the infrastructure and maintenance investment made by the network operator, mostly via its subcontractors, customers of the group
  • In the vast majority of cases, the group is referenced as sole supplier for the given operator for these 4 product families
  • The two product families 5 and 6, are proprietary to ETC Group ; principally non-referenced with the operator, but represent ETC Group’s offer as a one-stop shop
  • Such products are sold under ETC Group’s own label or sold by as sole national distributor for a foreign supplier of branded products
