Procurement as a Service

To anticipate mutation of Telco operators who need to focus on their core business to be competitive, ETC Group has developed a specific procurement model that combines expertise, technology and highly skilled ressources

Outsourcing process brings:

  • Lower material prices based on global volumes
  • Low working capital
  • No warehouse expenses
  • Prevents waste of material
  • Timely delivery and improved planning: help deploy faster

Logistics as a Service

Plug on play model specifically designed for telco operators. Our logistics experts are always looking for ways to do it better, faster and cheaper.

Logistics service covers:

  • Inbound / Outbound
  • Transportation network management
  • Warehouse management
  • Refurbishing (set-top box, home gateways)
  • Repair center
  • Mobile commercial activity (kitting, packaging, shipping)

Consultancy & Design

Telecommunication Market is facing major challenges today : new technologies, tough competition between Service Providers, business models involving large investments, evolution of consumer policy…

With 25+ years of experience in the Telecommunication Industry, ETC Group keeps providing the best Service to Telecom value chain by supporting all the actors to meet their business strategy.

Our offer:

Make sure Supply Chain & Operations meets the mark to improve top-line growth and minimize risks of wasting money and/or postpone rollout

Consultancy services in Product Development, Design & Innovation through a highly-skilled Technology team and with a long-term experience in telecommunication network projects

Ability to advise wisely customers on the right Engineering & Materials required to build efficiently a Network by reducing quantity of SKU’s, rollout & maintenance costs and risks on the whole Supply Chain

Training

To face increasing complexity of telecommunication networks, ETC Group has built a strong Technical team to support operators & contractors.

Both our Technical & sales team are constantly trained by global leading manufacturers to be fully aligned with latest technologies.

Our team can cover full Telecommunication requirements in the following areas:

  • FttH network rollout & maintenance
  • HFC upgrade & maintenance
  • 4G / 5G infra
  • Datacenters infra
  • B2B infra

Our Training capabilities:

  • On-site and webinars training
  • High level measurement equipment (Network monitoring,
  • Transmission testing, antenna alignment…)
  • Testing & Troubleshooting (Fiber, Copper, CATV)
  • Structured cabling design, engineering & installation
  • Fiber preparation and Fusion Splicing
